About Us!

Hello friends! Welcome to our website! We are the team behind Style Studio. Our team is made up of a variety of personalities from Girls Who Code. We created this website because we believe girls can spend way to much time deciding on what they are going to wear. Hopefully with our website, girls will be able to find a style they feel most comfortable in and get an idea of what they can wear. Our website showcases various different styles and explains the life of new fashion these days. This website was coded by Martha D , Daniela A , Katelyn P , Vivian C, and Serenity D using html and css. Check out our own profies and lookbooks down below!

Serenity D

My name is Serenity and I’m a Senior. For the style studio quiz, I got ‘tomboy’. Tomboy is a perfect way to describe me because I like wearing sporty clothes that aren’t feminine or hard to put on and take off. Wearing makeup made me feel pretty some moments, but other moments where the makeup burn my face or when skirts and dresses were too tight, I wanted to be comfortable for a while.
